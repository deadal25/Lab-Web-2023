About Gallery Fruit

Fruit is a natural product that comes from flowering plants and usually contains seeds. They come in a variety of shapes, sizes, colors and flavors. Fruit is generally rich of nutrients such as vitamins, fiber, minerals and antioxidants, making it a food choice healthy one. Examples of popular fruits include apples, oranges, dragon fruit, soursop, mango, kiwi, avocado, blueberries, cherries and many more. Fruits can be eaten fresh, processed into juice, made into jam, or used in various dishes and cakes. Fruit also has an important role in maintaining balance of natural ecosystems and in supporting plant pollination.
